Sample Analysis 2: Videogame Sales Dataset


Objectives
I had two objectives I sought to reach with this dataset. The first was to visualize the number of game sales per platform so that the relative popularity of each can be easily seen. The second was to gauge the dependence of sales from both user and critic reviews by attempting to establish a predictive model for sales based on review data using linear regression.
